ABOUT US

Welcome to Cardamon Bay Restaurant and Lounge in Rivenhall, Witham, CM8 3HB, the perfect destination for a delightful dining experience. Our elegant restaurant is designed to make your moments truly memorable.

Every morning from 7 AM to 10 AM, we serve a fresh and delicious breakfast to start your day right. Whether you're here for a relaxing lunch or an enjoyable evening with family and friends, Cardamon Bay Restaurant and Lounge is the perfect choice.
